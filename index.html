Case Study 1

Process
 
Prototype
!Issues:   Incomplete patient health records were scattered over numerous medical EHR systems and difficult to obtain for patients, families and doctors, lacking portability and making healthcare less efficient and effective
Process:   Defined the challenge, determined user goals and needs, empathy fieldwork, user analysis, brainstorming, wireframing, prototyping, UI design, user testing
 Results:     Our system provides one cohesive location for patient lifetime records, easing burdens for patients and families, while providing complete patient health history to doctors
